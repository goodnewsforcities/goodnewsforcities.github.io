Based in Japan, we are a pair of urbanites who have been working on collective actions regarding community development and citizen empowerment for reimaging of cities. Our field of interest is grassroots urban initiative, tactical urbanism, urban pedagogy, and community development.

Our goal is to create an international platform for young urbanists, designers, architects and artists with direct interests in cities across Asia, to enhance collaboration and share & arhive urban solutions in our increasingly globalized, connected world. The platform aims to connect and train actors from nonprofits, private enterprises and public institutions in Asia, then bring them into a peer network where they can exchange, inspire and learn from each other.
